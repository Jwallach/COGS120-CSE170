Welcome to GameBase!

We're a site dedicated to helping you find your next favorite game! Taking a survey of your preferences, along with feedback of past games you've played, we help find the perfect game for you.


Our site works by using aspects, which are similar to tags but encompass a more in depth categorization of a given game. For example, 2 games might both be classified as first person shooters, but one might be a casual party game while another might be a serious competitive skill based exercise. Aspects help people find games that are desgned in ways they enjoy, letting one find games with less obvious aspects such as good mod support, hard but fair gameplay, and games where creativity is encouraged. Aspects help one understand the philsophy behind a game without having to actually play it or gather testimony from other players (or worse, read the often meaningless descriptions or reviews by people who have barely played the game for a few hours)
